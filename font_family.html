A paragraph is a distinct section of writing, usually starting on a new line with an indent, that groups related sentences around a single central idea or topic, serving as a fundamental building block for organizing longer texts like essays and articles for better readability and structure. Typically, a paragraph includes a topic sentence, supporting sentences with details or examples, and sometimes a concluding sentence that bridges to the next paragraph, helping readers follow the flow of thought.
